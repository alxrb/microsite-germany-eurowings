Stuttgart is our German destination in focus, presenting an opportunity to get under the skin of a city that offers a remarkable selection of diverse experiences.

From high art to luxury cars, Stuttgart knows how to flaunt its wealth. Whether it’s exploring the postmodern Porsche museum, or exploring boutiques and galleries, Stuttgart wears luxury on its sleeve. Discover the vine-clad hills that surround the city, diving into rustic wine bars before heading to the centre for refined dining options. Hunt for treasures in the art nouveau market or head for the heavens with a visit to the Fernsehturm, where unparalleled panorama awaits. A city of palaces and renowned cultural institutions, Stuttgart offers traditional Swabian charms in bucket loads. Discover it here.

Stuttgart is a hotbed of culture and art, with a rich heritage that embellishes the grandiose palaces and fills its many galleries with fascinating works. Yet there is also plenty of modernity, borne out of a rich industrial heritage. From renowned ballet to modernist estates, Stuttgart offers a diverse cultural map.

Stuttgart’s most famous export is the motor car. The iconic roundels of Mercedes-Benz and Porsche are recognised the world over and, quite remarkably, they both reside in Stuttgart. Discover a history of landmark design and engineering in the veritable home of the automobile – though of course, that’s not all that’s on offer!
